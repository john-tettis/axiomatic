Axiomatic

Axiomatic is a project developed by me, John Tettis. At the time of this project, I am studying at Springboard as a software engineer. Axiomatic is the first of three Capstone projects to be completed in my time at Springboard.

My goals with axiomatic is to demonstrate my skills in web development while also producing a useful application for thinkers and contemplators. The API I used posed significant limitations to my design, as no search, category, or quote identification were provided. Although, this limitation ultimately made me more creative, and I am very pleased with the final design of Axiomatic.
